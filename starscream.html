Starscream

Starscream is a hard believer in tough love. One look from him will put any child in their place. Just like Optimus, Starscream is able to give children rides to and from school and can teach them techniques for piloting military fighter jets. His hatred of Megatron and thirst for power make him a great addition to our daycare service.
